Licensing

What is licensing?

Licensing is a commercial agreement where the owner of an intellectual property (IP) allows another party to use, produce, or sell products or services based on that IP, usually in exchange for a fee or royalties. It can take many forms such as clothing, video games, collectibles, toys, etc. The idea is to create a win-win scenario where:

  • consumers enjoy products or experiences tied to their favorite IPs,
  • licensees capitalize on established popularity,
  • and IP owners gain new revenue while strengthening their brand's identity and notoriety.
